# Product Vision

## Mission

Build the most developer-friendly booking platform that allows anyone to schedule, manage, and cancel appointments with zero friction.

## Target Users

- Customers booking appointments online
- Business owners managing their availability
- Support agents handling cancellations and reschedules

## Core Value Propositions

- One-click booking from any device
- Real-time availability with no double-booking
- Instant confirmation via email and SMS
- Seamless cancellation and refund process

## Success Metrics

- Booking completion rate > 90%
- Cancellation processing time < 30 seconds
- Payment success rate > 98%
- Customer satisfaction score > 4.5/5
